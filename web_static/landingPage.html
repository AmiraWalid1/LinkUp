Key Features

Discover People

Allows users to find and connect with new people based on their interests, location, or mutual connections. Users can browse profiles, send friend requests, and view suggested connections.

Activity Feed

The Activity Feed displays recent activities of the user's connections, such as posts, likes, and comments. It helps users stay updated with what their friends and connections are sharing and doing on the platform.

Content Sharing and Reactions

Allows users to share posts, photos, videos, and links on their timeline. Other users can react to these posts with likes, comments, and shares, promoting interaction and engagement.

About Linkup

The inspiration for Linkup came from our personal experiences with existing social platforms. We wanted to create a space where connecting with others is both simple and secure.
